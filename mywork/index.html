Flux

Flux is a excellent discord bot with some awsome fetures.

The flux site was made by me from the ground up and took me 2-3 days on and off the job.


Flux has mod fetures such as ban and kick, that leave logs, it also has some fun commands so you dont get bored. It also has a say command so you can make your server look clean.

It is aways online unless it is having updates, Flux's ping is also very good on an average of 10-20ms.

It also has a very helpful help page in discord (that gets sent to your DMs) we also offer support in the support server and in the live chat on the site.


To sum it up Flux is the bot made by you and for you.
